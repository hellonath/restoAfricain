Welcome to the Villa
its a restaurant that is located in Créteil, with an African Menu catalogue we welcome you at 9 rue Marc Seguin, to offer a culinary stroll to your taste buds and create gastronomic and gourmet memories!, it is the largest African restaurant in the capital with 300 seats. But it is also a unique place where we offer a unique experience and a journey on the African continent. It also has a chic and refined decoration, animal masks, wallpapers with the theme of Africa, get ready for a gustatory safari EXperience! The restaurant is open from Mondays to friday for lunch and dinner with different menus. In the heart of the African Villa, the spirit of the party is present! A lounge bar is available with an intimate space for events on the second floor and a sublime cocktail bar with African scents (Bissap is a famous African drink). Between modernity and authenticity, it is a place that will plunge you into the heart of African lands.

An African cooking in the spotlight
The African Villa offers you the possibility to taste halal African dishes: Mafé, Yassa, Tiep Bou Dienn, gombo sauce, peanut sauce ... You will taste African dishes which are home-made and elaborated by a team passionate about African cuisine. A colorful and tasty farandole awaits you in the heart of the restaurant. With a different menu for lunch and dinner, it's time to fall in love with this African cuisine. It is a crossroads of several culinary cultures of African lands.

The cooking at the African Villa
is a real mix of flavors, colors and ingredients! It is in the heart of Créteil that you take a direct flight to African lands in search of all the most delicious and gourmet dishes: Pastels, Tiep Bou Yap, Mafé, Yassa... With formulas for lunch, "à la carte" dishes for dinner and a menu for groups, African cuisine is waiting for you! Dear customers, we are waiting for you you will be inspired by an eclectic menu!
